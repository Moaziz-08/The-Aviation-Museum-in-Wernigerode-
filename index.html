Aviation Museum in Wernigerode

Discover the Fascinating World of Aviation

Explore the World of Aviation

The Aviation Museum in Wernigerode offers an exciting experience with over 50 real planes and helicopters. Discover an impressive collection of pilot helmets, engines, and detailed cockpit models. The museum’s expansive halls allow you to get up close to these amazing aircraft, making it a must-see for aviation enthusiasts.

Experience what it's like to sit in a real cockpit and feel like a pilot!

Interactive Flight Simulator

Take control in the museum’s flight simulator, where you can fly a plane or helicopter. Afterward, receive a special certificate to remember your experience as a pilot.

Rare Aircraft Collection

The museum showcases rare aircraft, including a jet flown by Prince Charles and a royal helicopter. Discover incredible stories with the audioguide and pick up a souvenir from the museum shop.

Fun for the Whole Family

This museum is perfect for families. Kids will be thrilled by the planes and can learn while having fun. Friendly staff members are always available to help make your visit memorable.
