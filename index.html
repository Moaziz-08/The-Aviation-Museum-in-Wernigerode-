Aviation Museum in Wernigerode

Discover the Thrilling World of Aviation

Experience the Wonders of Aviation

The Aviation Museum in Wernigerode is a fun place with over 50 real planes and helicopters. Explore an impressive collection of pilot helmets, engines, and detailed cockpit models. The museum's spacious halls allow you to get up close to these fascinating aircraft.

Get inside a real cockpit and feel like a pilot!

Flight Simulator & Certificate

Try out the museum's flight simulator where you can fly a plane or helicopter. Afterward, you'll receive a special certificate to commemorate your flight experience.

Rare and Historical Aircraft

The museum houses unique planes, such as a jet once flown by Prince Charles and a royal helicopter. Enjoy the museum's audioguide for fascinating stories, and don't forget to visit the souvenir shop.

Family-Friendly Fun

The Aviation Museum is an excellent destination for families. Kids will love the planes and can learn while having a great time. The friendly staff is always ready to assist.
