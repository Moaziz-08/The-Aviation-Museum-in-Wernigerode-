Discover the Fascinating World of Aviation

Explore more than 50 real planes and helicopters, admire unique pilot helmets, engines, and cockpit models. The spacious halls allow you to get up close to the aircraft and even sit in a cockpit!

Feel the thrill of being a pilot – experience it firsthand!

Flight Simulator

Take control of a plane or helicopter in the museum's flight simulator. After your flight, receive a special certificate to commemorate the experience.

Rare Aircraft Collection

The museum features rare aircraft, including a jet flown by Prince Charles and a royal helicopter. Explore these fascinating pieces of history while using the museum's audioguide.

Perfect for Families

Bring your family to the museum for an educational and fun experience. Kids will love exploring the planes, and the friendly staff is happy to assist with any questions!
